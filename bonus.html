Cuba Cubita

Cubita descends from one of only two domesticated dogs at the time of the Spanish conquest of the Caribbean islands and México. When Columbus arrived in Cuba in 1492, his journal entries cited the presence of strange barkless dogs.

Some fun facts about Cuba Cubita

  2. Cuba Cubita attributes are a kind demeanor, an upbeat attitude. She’s extra friendly.
  3. Cuba Cubita's heritage embraces features of Cuban life and history spanning three millennia.
